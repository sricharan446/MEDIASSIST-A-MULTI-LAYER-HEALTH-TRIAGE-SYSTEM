Symptom: Fever
Description: Elevated body temperature above 37.5C (99.5F)
Causes: Infection, inflammation, heat exhaustion, medication reaction
When to see doctor: Temperature above 39.4C (103F), persists more than 3 days, or with severe symptoms

Symptom: Headache
Description: Pain in the head or face, can be throbbing, constant, sharp, or dull
Causes: Tension, migraine, dehydration, sinus infection, high blood pressure
When to see doctor: Sudden severe headache, with fever/stiff neck, after head injury, with vision changes

Symptom: Cough
Description: Reflex action to clear the throat and airways
Causes: Cold, flu, allergies, asthma, GERD, infection
When to see doctor: Persists more than 3 weeks, produces blood, with shortness of breath

Symptom: Fatigue
Description: Extreme tiredness that does not improve with rest
Causes: Lack of sleep, anemia, diabetes, thyroid disorders, depression
When to see doctor: Persists more than 2 weeks, with unexplained weight loss, or with other symptoms

Symptom: Chest Pain
Description: Discomfort in the chest area ranging from dull ache to sharp stabbing
Causes: Heart problems, lung conditions, digestive issues, muscle strain
When to see doctor: EMERGENCY - if sudden, severe, with shortness of breath, or radiates to arm/jaw

Symptom: Shortness of Breath
Description: Difficulty breathing or feeling of not getting enough air
Causes: Asthma, COPD, heart conditions, anxiety, anemia
When to see doctor: EMERGENCY - if sudden onset, severe, or with chest pain

Symptom: Dizziness
Description: Feeling lightheaded, unsteady, or sensation of spinning
Causes: Inner ear problems, low blood pressure, dehydration, anemia, medication
When to see doctor: Recurrent episodes, with chest pain, or if causing falls

Symptom: Nausea
Description: Sensation of unease in the stomach with urge to vomit
Causes: Food poisoning, viral infection, pregnancy, medication, motion sickness
When to see doctor: Persists more than 2 days, with severe abdominal pain, or signs of dehydration

Symptom: Abdominal Pain
Description: Pain anywhere between the chest and groin
Causes: Indigestion, gastritis, appendicitis, gallstones, kidney stones
When to see doctor: Severe pain, with fever, bloody stool, or inability to eat

Symptom: Joint Pain
Description: Discomfort, aching, or soreness in joints
Causes: Arthritis, injury, infection, autoimmune conditions, gout
When to see doctor: Severe swelling, redness, warmth, or inability to use joint

Symptom: Back Pain
Description: Pain in the upper, middle, or lower back
Causes: Muscle strain, poor posture, herniated disc, kidney problems
When to see doctor: With leg weakness, bladder issues, or after injury

Symptom: Skin Rash
Description: Change in skin color or texture, may include bumps, itching, or scaling
Causes: Allergies, infections, eczema, psoriasis, medication reaction
When to see doctor: Spreads rapidly, with fever, or covers large body area

Symptom: Sore Throat
Description: Pain, scratchiness, or irritation in the throat
Causes: Viral infection, bacterial infection (strep), allergies, dry air
When to see doctor: Severe pain, difficulty swallowing, or with high fever

Symptom: Runny Nose
Description: Excess drainage from the nose, clear or colored
Causes: Cold, allergies, sinus infection, flu
When to see doctor: Persists more than 10 days, or with facial pain

Symptom: Loss of Smell/Taste
Description: Reduced or absent ability to smell or taste
Causes: COVID-19, nasal polyps, sinus infection, head injury
When to see doctor: Sudden onset, or persists more than 2 weeks
