Disease: Hypertension (High Blood Pressure)

Definition:
Hypertension is a condition where blood pressure remains persistently elevated.

Normal BP:
- Normal: <120/80 mmHg
- Elevated: 120–129/<80
- Stage 1: 130–139/80–89
- Stage 2: ≥140/90

Symptoms:
- Often no symptoms
- Headache
- Dizziness
- Chest pain (in severe cases)

Risk Factors:
- Obesity
- High salt diet
- Smoking
- Stress
- Family history

Treatment:
- Reduce salt intake
- Regular exercise
- ACE inhibitors
- Beta blockers
- Diuretics

Complications:
- Stroke
- Heart attack
- Kidney failure
