Medicine: Paracetamol (Acetaminophen)
Class: Analgesic, Antipyretic
Uses: Fever, mild to moderate pain, headache
Common brands: Tylenol, Calpol, Dolo 650
Typical dosage: 500-1000mg every 4-6 hours, max 4g/day
Precautions: Avoid in liver disease, do not exceed recommended dose

Medicine: Ibuprofen
Class: NSAID (Non-Steroidal Anti-Inflammatory Drug)
Uses: Pain, fever, inflammation, arthritis
Common brands: Advil, Brufen, Nurofen
Typical dosage: 200-400mg every 4-6 hours
Precautions: Avoid in kidney disease, stomach ulcers, pregnancy

Medicine: Cetirizine
Class: Antihistamine
Uses: Allergies, hay fever, hives, itching
Common brands: Zyrtec, Alerid
Typical dosage: 10mg once daily
Precautions: May cause drowsiness, avoid alcohol

Medicine: Omeprazole
Class: Proton Pump Inhibitor (PPI)
Uses: Acid reflux, GERD, stomach ulcers
Common brands: Prilosec, Omez
Typical dosage: 20-40mg once daily before meals
Precautions: Long-term use may affect calcium absorption

Medicine: Metformin
Class: Antidiabetic (Biguanide)
Uses: Type 2 diabetes
Common brands: Glucophage, Glycomet
Typical dosage: 500-2000mg daily in divided doses
Precautions: Take with food, avoid in kidney disease

Medicine: Amlodipine
Class: Calcium Channel Blocker
Uses: High blood pressure, angina
Common brands: Norvasc, Amlod
Typical dosage: 5-10mg once daily
Precautions: May cause ankle swelling, avoid grapefruit

Medicine: Atorvastatin
Class: Statin (HMG-CoA reductase inhibitor)
Uses: High cholesterol, cardiovascular disease prevention
Common brands: Lipitor, Atorva
Typical dosage: 10-80mg once daily
Precautions: Take at night, monitor liver function

Medicine: Losartan
Class: ARB (Angiotensin Receptor Blocker)
Uses: High blood pressure, diabetic kidney disease
Common brands: Cozaar, Losartan
Typical dosage: 50-100mg once daily
Precautions: Avoid in pregnancy, monitor potassium levels

Medicine: Salbutamol (Albuterol)
Class: Bronchodilator (Beta-2 Agonist)
Uses: Asthma, COPD, bronchospasm
Common brands: Ventolin, Asthalin
Typical dosage: 2 puffs as needed, up to 4 times daily
Precautions: May cause tremor, palpitations

Medicine: Amoxicillin
Class: Antibiotic (Penicillin)
Uses: Bacterial infections (respiratory, ear, skin, urinary)
Common brands: Amoxil, Mox
Typical dosage: 250-500mg three times daily
Precautions: Penicillin allergy, complete full course

Medicine: Azithromycin
Class: Antibiotic (Macrolide)
Uses: Bacterial infections, respiratory, skin, STIs
Common brands: Zithromax, Azee
Typical dosage: 500mg day 1, then 250mg days 2-5
Precautions: May prolong QT interval

Medicine: Pantoprazole
Class: Proton Pump Inhibitor (PPI)
Uses: GERD, peptic ulcer, H. pylori treatment
Common brands: Protonix, Pan
Typical dosage: 20-40mg once daily
Precautions: Take before meals, avoid long-term high doses

Medicine: Diclofenac
Class: NSAID
Uses: Pain, inflammation, arthritis, muscle pain
Common brands: Voltaren, Voveran
Typical dosage: 50mg 2-3 times daily
Precautions: Avoid in heart disease, kidney disease

Medicine: Levocetirizine
Class: Antihistamine
Uses: Allergies, chronic urticaria
Common brands: Xyzal, Levosiz
Typical dosage: 5mg once daily
Precautions: Less drowsy than older antihistamines

Medicine: Montelukast
Class: Leukotriene Receptor Antagonist
Uses: Asthma, allergic rhinitis
Common brands: Singulair, Montair
Typical dosage: 10mg once daily at bedtime
Precautions: May cause mood changes, not for acute attacks
