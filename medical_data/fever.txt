Disease: Fever

Definition:
Fever is an elevation of body temperature above normal (98.6°F or 37°C).

Normal Temperature:
- 97°F – 99°F

Causes:
- Viral infection
- Bacterial infection
- Malaria
- Dengue
- COVID-19

Symptoms:
- Chills
- Sweating
- Headache
- Body pain
- Weakness

Treatment:
- Paracetamol
- Hydration
- Rest
- Treat underlying infection

When to see doctor:
- Fever above 103°F
- Fever lasting more than 3 days
- Difficulty breathing
